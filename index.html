About the Festival

Marl Pits Festival is a free, family-friendly community event celebrating local music, food, and activities in the heart of Rossendale. Everyone is welcome for a relaxed day of fun by the water.

What’s On

  • Live music and performances
  • Food and drink stalls
  • Children’s activities
  • Local community groups and charities

Where & When

Location: Marl Pits, Rossendale

Date: Saturday 2 May

Entry: Free
